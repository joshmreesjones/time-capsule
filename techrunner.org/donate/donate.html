Why donate?

Software is not free. Books to learn the software can add up. Computer upgrades to run the software are expensive. Hosting a website is usually a monthly or tri-monthly expense. Donations from people like you will:

  1. Keep TechRunner up and running
  2. Help me buy books, software, and computer parts
  3. Give content writers like me more knowledge to give you the best tutorials possible
  4. Expand the range of articles and tutorials to new areas
  5. Make you feel warm inside

No money collected from donating web surfers will be used in any way other than advancing the website and its content. In addition, a list of donors will be maintained on this page and each donor will get a small page of their own, unless a donor requests to be anonymous and/or does not want a page.
